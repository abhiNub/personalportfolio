Greetings and thank you for visiting the official portfolio website of Abhinav Pratap Singh, also known as Playsick Abhi. The webpage is designed around his most recent creation, Roz-Marra.
Within this site, you will discover direct access to his Spotify and social media accounts, as well as convenient links to some of his notable releases.



















Playsick Abhi, also known as Abhinav, is an engineering student at IIT Guwahati who has harbored a lifelong passion for artistic endeavors. Despite coming from a middle-class background, he has always desired to pursue something unique and creative. During his time in Kota preparing for the IIT-JEE exams in 2019, Abhinav discovered his love for songwriting. Although his parents had high expectations for his academic success, Abhinav aimed to make them proud by excelling in his studies and simultaneously pursuing his musical aspirations while in college. With his triumph in the JEE exam and acceptance into IIT Guwahati, Abhinav embarked on his musical journey by releasing his debut studio single, 'Playsick Intro.' Since then, he has continued to release songs of diverse genres and has exciting plans for his future in the music industry.
